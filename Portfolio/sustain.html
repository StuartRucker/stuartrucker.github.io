Website Features
  • The Front Page has hexagonal articles previews shaped into a tree
  • Articles can be searched by content, title, and authors at sustainexeter.org/search
  • Author pages, complete with pictures, descriptions, and all their articles. (e.g sustainexeter.org/author/prsanson)
  • Articles pages, with suggested articles to read next
  • Section pages, where articles are grouped by topic. (e.g sustainexeter.org/section/world)
Content Management System

After seeing how it took a long time to copy and paste articles into The Exonian's Wordpress Content Management System (CMS), I created a new CMS that syncs articles with Google Drive. At the top of each article, the author enter's information on the article in a set way: 

Title = Whatever article
Section = World
Authors = stuart rucker, john Doe

Then the writer selects the doc using the admin web interface, and the article is added. There is also a form to add Authors, which are linked to the articles
