Arduino Clock

Arduino, Adobe Illustrator, Laser-Cutter

This laser cut, Arduino powered clock provides has an aesthetically pleasing laser cut frame. It incorporates both anilog and digital features with 12 LED's lining the perimeter to denote hours, and a rotating wheel in the center to denote seconds. Using the control panel, the time can be adjusted, and an alarm can set.
